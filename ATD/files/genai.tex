During the creation of this document, we used generative artificial intelligence tools, specifically Large Language Models (LLMs). \\
We used the tools for the following purposes: \\
\textbf{Stylistic improvements}: We provided drafts of sections of the work as input and asked for suggestions to improve sentence structure, clarity of exposition, and the precision of technical terminology. \\
\textbf{Translation assistance}: Since English is not our native language, we used the LLMs to verify the correctness of the translation from Italian, in particular to check grammar and syntax. \\
All outputs generated by the AI tools were reviewed by both group members to ensure quality, consistency, and correctness.
